About Latecomer Tracker

Latecomer Tracker is an advanced attendance management system designed to track and monitor student arrivals in real-time. Our goal is to ensure punctuality and provide insightful data for academic institutions.

We are committed to creating a **smart, efficient, and reliable** solution that helps institutions maintain discipline while offering students transparency regarding their attendance records.
